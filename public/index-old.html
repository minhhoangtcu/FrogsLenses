Frogs Lenses is a web application designed to create different cultural perspectives through the use of pictures. This application is designed to help international students to adapt to culture shocks. When they first join TCU, there will be a lot of learning curve. We found out that there is not a lot of cultural material online or in book to help them through these hard time. Furthermore, most of these materials are very dry and none attractive. This application is a fun way for international students at TCU to share their culture perspective with their friends. Native American students will also have a chance to easily experience foreign culture.
